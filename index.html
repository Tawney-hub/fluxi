WHO WE ARE

Fluxi is a South African creative studio that blends software development (websites and mobile apps) and graphic design (branding and visuals) into one powerful service. Whether you need a clean website, a functional mobile app, or eye-catching branding, Fluxi makes your business look and perform better — online and offline.

Digital Solutions

We craft innovative and scalable software solutions, including responsive business websites, robust e-commerce platforms, and intuitive mobile applications for both Android and iOS.

Visual Storytelling

Our graphic design services help you create a compelling visual identity through custom logos, engaging social media content, impactful flyers, and professional video editing, ensuring your brand stands out.

Why Choose Fluxi?

We are a passionate team dedicated to delivering high-quality, creative, and effective solutions for your business. We prioritize your vision and work closely with you to bring it to life, ensuring seamless integration between your digital platforms and visual brand identity.
